## 2026-05-11 - Component Read-Only vs Interactive Accessibility
**Learning:** Components that serve dual purposes (both display-only and interactive forms) like Star Ratings require entirely different screen reader flows depending on their mode. In read-only mode, individual items should be hidden with `aria-hidden="true"` and summarized on the container using `role="img"` and `aria-label`. In interactive mode, the container needs no role, and individual items need their own `aria-label`, `aria-pressed`, and keyboard focus styles (`focus-visible:ring-2`).
**Action:** When creating components that have both a `readOnly` or `interactive` flag, split the ARIA tree logic at the container level to avoid screen readers announcing multiple disabled buttons when just trying to read a score or value.
