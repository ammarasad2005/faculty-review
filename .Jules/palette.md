## 2024-05-18 - Star Rating Read-only vs Interactive Accessibility\n**Learning:** When a component like Star Rating acts both as an input (interactive) and an indicator (read-only), screen reader expectations differ. In read-only mode, individual stars are redundant and should be hidden from accessibility trees, presenting a unified container summary (e.g., `role="img"` and `aria-label="3 out of 5 stars"`). In interactive mode, individual stars act as controls and require explicit labels and state (`role="group"`, `aria-label`, `aria-pressed`).\n**Action:** Apply state-dependent ARIA configurations to dual-purpose UI components instead of static accessibility markup.
